# Design Guidelines\n\n**Mindful** follows a **Modern & Clean** visual style, prioritizing a distraction-free and focused environment. The interface should be uncluttered, with a strong emphasis on typography, whitespace, and accessibility.\n\n## Design Style\n- **Modern & Clean:** Adheres to modern design principles, with a clean and professional look across Android.\n- **Visual Clarity:** Prioritize high contrast and clear visual hierarchy to help users focus on what's important.\n\n## Tone & Voice\n- **Professional & Efficient:** The app uses clear, direct, and efficient language to help users achieve their goals. It remains professional and avoids unnecessary filler or flowery prose.\n\n## UX Principles\n- **Offline-First Navigation:** Every feature should be accessible and usable without an internet connection.\n- **Task-Oriented Flow:** The app's navigation and interactions should be optimized for completing a single task at a time, minimizing distractions within the app itself.\n- **Intuitive Interactions:** Use standard platform patterns to ensure a familiar and effortless experience.
