# Initial Concept\n\nMindful is a privacy-first, open-source Android app designed to help users regain control over their digital habits, improve focus, and boost productivity. It caters to **Digital Minimalists** and **Privacy Enthusiasts** by providing a strict set of guardrails to block and restrict distracting apps while keeping all data local and secure.\n\n## Vision\nTo provide the most robust and privacy-respecting toolset for anyone seeking to minimize digital distractions and reclaim their time.\n\n## Target Audience\n- **Digital Minimalists:** People struggling with digital addiction or high screen time.\n- **Privacy Enthusiasts:** Users who prioritize local-first, open-source software and want to ensure no data leaves their device.\n\n## Core Features\n- **Strict Guardrail Blocking:** Robust app and feature blocking to ensure distractions are kept at bay.\n- **Focus Mode:** Dedicated sessions for study, work, or creative tasks.\n- **Screen Time Limits:** Daily usage limits with "Invincible Mode" to lock restrictions.\n- **Detailed Usage Insights:** Offline-first data visualization of screen time and app usage.\n- **Privacy-First & FOSS:** No ads, no tracking, completely offline, and open-source forever.\n\n## Unique Selling Points\n- **Privacy-First:** All data stays on the device.\n- **FOSS Nature:** Community-driven and transparent development.\n- **Blocking Excellence:** A focus on strict app and feature restrictions to prevent relapse.
